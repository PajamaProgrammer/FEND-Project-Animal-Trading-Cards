Narwhal

The unicorn of the sea!

  • Scientific Name: Monodon monoceros
  • Average Length: 17 feet
  • Average Lifespan: 50 years
  • Habitat: The Arctic Seas

The Narwhal is a type of whale that lives in the icy waters of the Arctic Seas. The narwhal tusk - found on most males and roughly 15% of females - is actually an enlarged tooth. The spiraled tusk juts from the head and can grow as long as 10 feet. It has a tough core and soft outer layer that can bend significantly without cracking.
